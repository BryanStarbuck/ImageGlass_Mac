# Releases & News

ImageGlass continues to ship regular feature and bugfix releases on its
v9 branch while simultaneously developing v10 — a full cross-platform
rewrite.

## Current Stable

**ImageGlass 9.5.0.515** — stable, Windows.

## Recent Releases (Reverse Chronological)

### ImageGlass 9.5 — May 2026
A minor update delivering "community-requested features and bugfixes."
Continues the v9 maintenance line.

### ImageGlass 10 Beta 1 — March 2026
The first public beta of the next major version. Highlights:

- Complete rewrite using **modern .NET**.
- UI built on the **Avalonia UI** framework.
- Targets **multiple platforms from a single, unified codebase** —
  ImageGlass is no longer Windows-only.
- This is **beta software** — v9 remains the recommended stable release
  for everyday use.

### ImageGlass 9.3 — 15-year anniversary
Marked 15 years of active development. Headline change: enhanced
**Windows Explorer compatibility** so ImageGlass behaves more like a
first-class native viewer when invoked from the shell.

### ImageGlass 9.2
- **Image Resize tool** added.
- **Crop tool** upgraded.

### ImageGlass 9.1
- **Lossless compression** added.
- **Startup Boost** added for faster perceived launch.

  funding platform.
- **2025 — 15-year anniversary**: celebrated with the 9.3 release.

## End of Life

- The **8.x series** is no longer receiving updates. Users on 8.x are
  encouraged to upgrade to 9.x (stable) or try 10 Beta.

## Roadmap

A 2026 roadmap update outlined the transition from v9 to v10. Key
themes:

1. **Cross-platform parity** — bring all v9 features to Avalonia.
2. **Single codebase** — replace the WinForms-era foundations.
3. **Community transparency** — public roadmap, public betas, public
   discussion.

The maintainer has signaled that v9 will continue to receive maintenance
releases throughout the v10 beta period so users aren't forced to
upgrade until v10 reaches stable.
